# Tableau-HW
Citi-Bike-Analytics

A analysis for the New York Citi Bike Program, in which responsible for overseeing the largest bike sharing program  in the United States in order to generate business insights in terms of visulize the peak time in both summer and winter period and the top start location in New York City and Jersey City, New Jersey

Findings

(1) The current major citi bike riders fall into young male group between 18 -20 but number of female riders increases over time as they are showing interest to start riding during the weekend

(2) Trips ended mainly in Manhattan.

(3) As the temperature gets cold as winter begins, people tend not to ride as well because of the lack of comfort individuals face when riding in low temperatures. Therefore, at some point the ridership does not grow.
